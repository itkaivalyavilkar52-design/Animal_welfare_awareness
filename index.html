What is Animal Welfare?

Animal welfare refers to the humane treatment of animals and ensuring that they live healthy, safe, and dignified lives. It includes proper food, shelter, medical care, and protection from cruelty or neglect.

Major Animal Welfare Issues

Stray Animals

Many animals suffer due to hunger, disease, and road accidents.

Animal Cruelty

Physical abuse, neglect, and illegal practices cause serious harm.

Lack of Awareness

People are often unaware of laws and basic animal care.

Animal Protection Laws in India

  • Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960
  • Animal Welfare Board of India (AWBI) Guidelines
  • Citizens have a duty to show compassion towards animals
